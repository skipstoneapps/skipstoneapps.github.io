GridSpace

Diagramless Crossword Puzzles

GridSpace is a crossword app where the grid starts empty. You aren't just solving clues — you're deducing the structure of the grid itself. It's a spatial logic puzzle disguised as a crossword, designed for those who enjoy the challenge of building the map while they navigate it.

  • 15 free puzzles across 3 difficulty levels (7x7, 9x9, and 11x11 grids)
  • Weekly Spotlight puzzles with unique themes
  • Relaxed and Timed game modes
  • Two visual themes: Cosmic (dark) and Classic (light)
  • Additional puzzle packs and hint packs available via in-app purchase
